Infosys

Infosys Limited is an Indian multinational information technology giant that delivers global business consulting, software engineering, and digital outsourcing solutions. Established on July 2, 1981,by N. R. Narayana Murthy and six visionary engineers with a meager $250 startup capital, the company relocated its primary hub to Bengaluru, India, and grew into a pillar of the Indian tech ecosystem. Guided by Chairman Nandan Nilekani and CEO Salil Parekh, the enterprise serves clients across 59 countries through an expansive grid of nearly 329,000 employees. Financially solid, the company's annual revenue has crossed the $20 billion mark, anchored by high-impact core solutions like the Finacle core banking framework and an "AI-first" modernization blueprint driving systems like Infosys Topaz.
